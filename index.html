PEACE FOR ROHINGYAS

Political Activism, Relief and Institutional Startups

Defending Human Rights Violations of Minorities By Governments


Text "PARIS" to 484848

Problem

Ronhingya men are being burned alive. Countless women are being raped and set on fire in locked houses. Babies are being slaughtered or thrown into the fire in front of their mothers. Nowhere in the world do they burn people alive, except in Burma. All are being committed by Burmese military and local mobs.

Read More
Solution

Congress is now considering House and Senate bills (H.R. 4223 & S. 2060) that will:

  • Prohibit US military-to-military cooperation with Burmese military
  • Facilitate humanitarian assistance
  • Mandate US government to take steps to end the persecution and ethnic cleansing
  • Implement targeted sanctions against the Burmese military

Our campaign will facilitate the public to demand lawmakers and President Trump to:

  • Co-sponsor or support The Burmese Human Rights and Freedom Act (S. 2060) and The Burma Unified Through Rigorous Military Accountability Act (H.R. 4223)
  • Demand full sanctions on Burmese military government as targeted sanctions are difficult to implement and will not deter the Burmese military. Full sanctions will also prohibit US companies from dealing with businesses associated with the Burmese military
What we are doing:

Bottom up pressure grassroots mobilization steps:

  1. We recruit volunteers.
  2. Our volunteers recruit supporters who text PARIS to 484848 to join the campaign.
  3. We text our supporters to ask if they are ready to talk to their lawmaker or the White House.
  4. When supporter is ready, we automatically send a phone call to the supporter, connecting the supporter to their lawmaker or the White House.
  5. Supporter says name, address and that they support bill S.2060 and full sanctions on Burmese military.
  6. We measure how many successful calls were made to lawmaker's office.

Top down pressure -- Leadership Mobilization steps:

  1. We recruit political, business and community leaders to join the campaign.
  2. We Schedule meetings with lawmakers to co-sponsor or support and strengthen the bills in the house and senate.

The Peace for Rohingya campaign strategy is to put top down and bottom up pressure on lawmakers and the White House to take legislative action to stop the brutal billing and persecution.

Experience
  • PARIS, has in-depth experience in running successful national campaigns. We've recruited thousands of volunteers using Harvard professor Marshall Ganz's organizing model and advanced technology.
  • This is a humanitarian issue, not just a local political or religious issue. Let's train and equip our boots on the ground to mobilize the public.
