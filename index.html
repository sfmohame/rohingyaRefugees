PEACE FOR ROHINGYAS

To defend human rights violations of minorities by governments & provide relief to mitigate the resulting refugee crisis.

  • Experiences
  • Volunteers

Problem

Ronhingya men are being burned alive. Countless women are being raped and set on fire in locked houses. Babies are being thrown into the fire in front of their mothers. Nowhere in the world do they burn people alive, except in Burma. All are being committed by Burmese military and local mobs.

  • STOP Military Assistance

    US needs to stop military to military assistance for Burmese.

  • Economic Sanctions

    US needs to put back economic sanctions on Burma.

  • Sending Troops

    US needs to lead UN in sending troops to defend Rohingyas.

WHAT ARE WE DOING:

TAKE ACTION TO STOP THE BRUTALITY!

• Peace for Rohingyas is a nation-wide campaign launched by P-A-R-I-S to mobilize the public to make thousands of CALLS (NOT EMAILS) to their lawmakers so they take action to stop the brutality.

• PARIS will mobilize coalitions of political, community, business and religious leaders to meet with lawmakers for their support for legislative action.  

• Most people have given humanitarian assistance for Rohingya refugees. Now to stop the brutality against Rohingyas, PARIS will lead the much needed legislative action.
